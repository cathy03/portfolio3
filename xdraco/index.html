DSP : DRACO STAKING PROGRAM

All MIR 4 users can participate in DSP by staking DRACOs in one or more active staking rounds to become eligible for a chance to win XDRACO item draws at the end of each staking round schedule.

In each staking round, the winner is randomly selected based on individual participant winning odds formulated through the fair-time staking algorithm. All participants may increase their winning odds by staking more DRACOs for a longer period of time.

The randomly selected winner will receive the XDRACO item in exchange for the total number of DRACOs staked for the specific round by the winning participant. DRACOs collected from the winner will be proportionately distributed to all other participants in the same staking round that did not win the draw as a staking participation bonus.

CHARACTER NFT

Character NFT is an innovative service offering you the freedom to tokenize your most important game asset, your character.

Tokenized characters are unbound from their game accounts and truly become assets existing on the blockchain. All character data, which makes the character unique in the first place, will be stored on a distributed ledger WEMIX. Character NFT grants you true ownership over your game chracters. In addtion to this, tokenized characters can be purchased, sold, or traded with WEMIX CREDIT through the WEMIX Wallet. The transcation process is fully decentralized, transferring power and authority to players.
